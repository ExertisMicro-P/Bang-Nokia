APPS FOR JUST ABOUT EVERY BUSINESS TASK

There are more apps than ever to help business become more productive. With the right smartphone or tablet and some helpful apps, it's now far easier to work effectively on the move - great news for those travelling, or out with clients on a regular basis.

Evernote

In the dash from office to meeting and back again, it's easy to lose track of what you are working on. Evernote is an easy-to-use app that helps you remember everything across all of your devices. You can create a plain text note on your tablet, and then open it on your smartphone or any other devices you use. It's simple and ideal for helping busy entrepreneurs keep track of their next big idea.

Office Mobile

Built-in Office Mobile means you can open, edit and save documents in Word, Powerpoint, Excel and OneNote, anytime, anywhere without losing original formatting.

Expensify

Expensify will help you keep track of your team's business expenses and mileage, while letting you scan and upload receipts. With just one click of a button, users can even file receipts by trip and submit expense reports to yourself or a client.

HERE Maps and HERE Drive

HERE Maps and HERE Drive will ensure you are never late to a meeting. No matter how you choose to travel. HERE Maps will show you the smartest, speediest way across town. You can download maps so you don't need to worry about network connection. And HERE Drive gives you fast, responsive, turn-by-turn voice-guided navigation.
